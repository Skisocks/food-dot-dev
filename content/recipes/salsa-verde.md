## Instructions

1. Chop the herbs and garlic, mixing with sea salt, until very fine.
2. Add the mustard and capers, and combine. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and stir in the olive oil.
3. Add the vinegar, little by little, stirring and tasting as you go.
