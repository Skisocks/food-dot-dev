## Instructions

1. Chop up all the vegetables, apart from the tenderstem broccoli. 
2. Place them into a bowl and add olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic, and the cumin. 
3. Separate the rosemary and thyme leaves from the sprigs and sprinkle them over the vegetables. Drizzle the honey over the vegetables. 
4. Toss everything together to ensure the vegetables are well coated.
5. Place vegetables on baking tray and roast in the oven at 180 for 40 minutes, stirring the vegetables halfway.
6. Serve with your favourite choice of protein and carbs :)
